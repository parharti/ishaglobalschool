From the Correspondent's Desk

Isha Global School was born from a deep question: Can education be free from fear, pressure, and comparison? Can it nurture children who are not just academically capable, but emotionally grounded, globally aware, and inwardly wise?

We are not a conventional school. We are here to raise human beings who think clearly, feel deeply, and live responsibly. Inspired by the profound vision of J. Krishnamurti, learning at Isha flows through conversations, questions, reflections, silence, and observation. There is no ranking, no competition, no forced learning — only a space where children can grow with joy, awareness, and inner freedom.

From Japan, we bring the art of cultivating independence and joyful discipline. From Finland, we integrate emotional wellbeing and creativity into education. From Reggio Emilia, we embrace inquiry-based, child-led learning. This is a school where each child is seen, heard, and valued — not for what they achieve, but for who they truly are.

Dear parents, choosing Isha is choosing depth over speed, connection over competition, and purpose over performance. It is choosing to raise children who are not just successful — but whole.

Dr. Subramaniyan Kuppusamy MBBS., MD. Correspondent — Arumbu Educational Trust Isha Global School, Kadampuliyur
