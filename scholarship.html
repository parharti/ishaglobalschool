Little Stars Scholarship Programme

By Arumbu Educational Trust in association with Isha Global School

We Believe Every Child Deserves Better Foundational Education

Financial hardship should never stand between a child and quality education. The Little Stars Scholarship ensures no deserving child is ever turned away from Isha Global School.

⭐ Non-profit · Community-supported · Transparent

Who Can Apply?

  • Parent or guardian who cannot afford the child's educational expenses
  • Child must be of primary school age eligible for IGS admission
  • Family residing in or around Kadampuliyur
  • Must submit the official scholarship application form
  • Supporting documents on financial status may be required
  • Preference given to single-parent households and BPL families

Note: If you are unsure whether you qualify, please still apply or call us at 9150720351. We are here to help.

No Child Left Behind

The Arumbu Educational Trust was established on the conviction that a child's potential should never be limited by their parents' income.
