Inquiry Based Learning

Learning through exploration and questioning — not memorisation.

"Everything must be questioned. Then the mind becomes extraordinarily sensitive, alive and intelligent." — J. Krishnamurti

The 5-Step IBL Process

  • ASK — Children frame their own questions
  • INVESTIGATE — Hands-on exploration & research
  • CREATE — Build models, projects & ideas
  • DISCUSS — Share findings with peers
  • REFLECT — Journal & self-assess their learning

Student-centred, real-life ready, and focused on independent thinking — not memorising textbooks.

🕊️

Freedom from Fear

No stress, no marks pressure — children learn best when they feel safe.

"To understand life is to understand ourselves, and that is both the beginning and the end of education." — J. Krishnamurti

Say NO to

  • Stress & Anxiety
  • Fear of Failure
  • Marks Pressure
  • Suppressed opinions

Say YES to

  • Explore freely
  • Express views & opinions
  • Learn from setbacks
  • Grow with confidence

When fear goes down, the ability to express goes up.
